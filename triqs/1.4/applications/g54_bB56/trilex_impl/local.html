Local functions¶

These functions allow to compute local diagrams such as \(\Sigma=\lambda GW \Lambda\), \(P=\lambda GG \Lambda\), or the three-point vertex out of the three-point function...

The high-level function which is used to go from the three-point function to the three-point vertex after the solution of an impurity problem is evaluate_vertex.

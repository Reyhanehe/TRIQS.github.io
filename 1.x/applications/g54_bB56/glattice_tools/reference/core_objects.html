Core objets¶

The main objets to perform a many-body calculation are
  • functions of space fct_on_mesh
  • functions of space and time glattice
To define such functions, one needs
  • the notion of domains (e.g. brillouin_zone_t)
  • the notion of mesh (e.g. a discrete mesh on a Brillouin zone, BZ_mesh)
This library also provides general functions to many-body computations:
  • Fourier transforms (on \(i\omega\) (fourier_freq) and \(\mathbf{k}\) (fourier_space))
  • summations over the Brillouin zone (get_local_part)

All objects are implemented at the C++ level. Some are wrapped in python.
